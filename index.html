About

Leave It Here is not a timeline.

Leave It Here is a calm anonymous room for feelings that need somewhere to go. There are no profiles, followers, likes, comments, or popularity signals. Shared posts are simple anonymous notes, shown softly and filterable by mood. Comfort is limited to a few kind phrases so the room never turns into a battlefield.
