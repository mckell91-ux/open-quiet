Community Guidelines

Keep the room quiet and safe.

Leave It Here is for emotional release, not harm, targeting, or explicit unsafe content.

Not allowed

  • Threats or encouragement of violence.
  • Harassment, targeted abuse, or intimidation.
  • Doxxing, private contact details, addresses, or identifying information.
  • Hate toward protected groups or dehumanizing attacks.
  • Spam, scams, repeated promotion, or attempts to manipulate the space.
  • Sexual content involving minors.

Use the report button when a shared post breaks these guidelines. Reports are local in this static demo and are structured for future moderation tools.
